Your Destination:
A Deserted Island

You applied for the Getaway Package. You will fly to a deserted island. We don't know the name yet, it's up to you to choose it!

You have four options to choose from for your island, pick wisely! Do you want a heart shaped pond? Or maybe you prefer an east facing dock? The choice is yours!

You get a tent all to yourself! Choose where you want to place it. Do you want to relax on the beach? Would you prefer a lake? Maybe a cliff? And don't worry, you'll get the chance to upgrade your tent to a house in the future.
