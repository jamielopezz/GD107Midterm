A Guide to Animal Crossing: New Horizons

In Animal Crossing: New Horizons, you'll travel to a deserted island where it is up to you to develop and customize your island for yourself and your residents!

Meet Timmy and Tommy who will send you on your way to a deserted island that you can customize as your own!

Get directions from Tom Nook on how to clean up your island!

Meet new villagers and invite them to live on your island!

Customize your house, expand, and explore your new island!
