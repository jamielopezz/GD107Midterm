Meet the Characters

There are different animals on the island for you to meet!

Timmy, Tommy, and Tom Nook are racoons who work on your island. Timmy and Tommy work at Nook's Cranny, which is a store with furniture, tools, and much more! Tom Nook works at Resident Services, where he will help you with upgrading your home, paying off your mortgage, etc.

Isabelle also lives on your island. Isabelle is a hard worker who wants to make sure your island is perfect! She will be your secretary and your assistant.

The Able Sisters are a trio of porcupines who work at the Able Sisters clothing store where you can buy their clothes and accessories! Their sister Label, on the top, is a fashion designer who you will get to meet as well.

There are so many other characters for you to befriend along the way!
